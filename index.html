    Pistols

    A pistol is primarily a secondary weapon that can be wielded in one hand or dual wielded commonly called akimbo .

    SMG

    a primary weapon that has high mobility best used at short to medium distances.

    Shotgun

    A primary weapon thats often a one shot kill at close range. best used at close distances

    Assault rifle

    A Primary weapon that has good accuracy and mobility best used at medium to long range distances.

    RPG

    A RPG is a secondary weapon best suited for taking out air craft.

    sniper rifle

    A primary weapon used best at long ranges.

    shield

    A ballistic shield that protects you gun fire.

    knife

    standard issue combat knife for quick melee kills. theres also a ballistics knife thats capable of launching the blade a good distance.

    grenade

    A grenade is a hand thrown explosive. theres the traditional frag grenade, semtex grenades that sticks to surfaces, and concussion grenades

    LMG

    A powerful light machine gun with a high rate of fire and high capacity magazine. capable of wall banging enemies behind cover.

    launcher

    typically mounted under the barrel of a shotgun or assault rifle it uses 40mm explosive rounds .
